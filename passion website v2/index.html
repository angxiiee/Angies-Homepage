Angie's Closet!

Welcome to my passion website!

My name is Angie and my passion is… fashion!

When I was in middle school, I used to hate clothes shopping because we would always shop at one store and I never found anything I liked. This made it so my closet was filled with a bunch of clothes I hated and I would typically just wear the same outfit everyday.

When I was 15, I was able to get my first job. At first, I didn’t really know what to do with all the money I was making, so I ended up just saving it all. Eventually, I started going to the mall, and that’s when my addiction for shopping started. At first, I was too scared to buy anything, so I would just go into a bunch of stores and try on different clothing items. Eventually, I started purchasing clothes for myself, and evolved a new style that suited me better than before. Going to the mall slowly became a hobby of mine, and I found myself there at least once a week.

When I went away to college, unfortunately, going to the mall became quite difficult because there was no mall nearby. With my shopping addiction requiring a solution, I turned to a new way of shopping: online shopping. Online shopping soon became my new hobby in order to continue my addiction. Since then, I have now become addicted to both online shopping and regular shopping and so I barely have any money.
